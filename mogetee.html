The Company

A Global Boba Brand, Racing to Keep Up with Itself

Möge Tee is a premium boba tea brand founded in mainland China, built on the promise of freshness and high-quality natural ingredients. With locations spanning North America, Australia, and Southeast Asia, the brand had grown into a global name — but its US presence was expanding faster than its marketing could follow.

By the time I came in, MögeTee had over 50 active franchise locations across the United States, with new stores opening regularly. The product was strong. The brand recognition among boba enthusiasts was real. But the marketing infrastructure hadn't caught up — and they needed someone to build it while the train was already moving.

The Challenge

50 Locations, Zero Unified Strategy

Rapid US expansion had created a fragmented brand experience. Each franchise was operating independently — and the gaps were starting to show.

No Marketing Uniformity

With franchises opening across the country at speed, there was no centralized guidance on messaging, promotions, or brand standards — leading to inconsistent customer experiences.

Weak Social Media Presence

Despite a globally recognized product, MögeTee's US social accounts had low follower counts and minimal engagement — limiting their ability to reach new customers online.

No Influencer or Paid Ad Strategy

The brand had no existing influencer relationships and had not run structured paid ad campaigns — leaving significant growth potential untapped.

Franchise Coordination Gaps

Seasonal and promotional campaigns had no delivery system to reach all locations consistently — meaning individual stores missed out on national promotions.

The Approach

Four Pillars of Execution

01

Build an Influencer Network from Scratch

Identified and outreached to 30+ micro-influencers across Instagram who aligned with MögeTee's audience — food lovers, lifestyle creators, and local community figures. Rather than expensive paid partnerships, I structured deals around commission and free product, keeping costs minimal while generating authentic, high-reach content. The result: 3,000 new Instagram followers in just 4 months, alongside a measurable lift in daily engagement.

📲 30+ influencer partners · 3,000+ new followers · 4-month timeline · low-cost model
02

Launch "Be the Mochi to my Berry" — Valentine's Campaign

Conceived and executed a Valentine's Day campaign centered around a new limited drink pairing — Mochi and Berry — framed as a love story between two flavors. The campaign had its own identity, messaging, and recipes, designed to be celebratory and shareable. I was responsible for the full franchise rollout — packaging the campaign into a ready-to-deploy kit and delivering it to every participating US location, ensuring a consistent, on-brand experience nationwide.

🧋 Original campaign concept · franchise-wide delivery · unified messaging + recipes
03

Launch & Optimize Paid Advertising

Proposed and launched MögeTee's first structured paid advertising effort on Facebook and Instagram. Ran A/B tests across multiple ad formats — static images, video, carousel — to identify what resonated most with the target audience. The data gathered directly informed future creative strategy, making each subsequent campaign more efficient and better targeted than the last.

📊 Facebook & Instagram Ads · A/B testing across ad formats · data-driven creative decisions
04

Develop Branded Merchandise for Store Activations

Sourced and developed a line of branded merchandise — keychains, figures, balloons, and flowers — designed for new store opening events, raffles, and seasonal promotions. Worked directly with manufacturers in China to design prototypes, review samples, and ensure quality before delivery. The merchandise created tangible brand moments at the point of experience, boosting in-store excitement and customer loyalty.

🎁 Keychains · figures · balloons · vendor-managed from prototype to delivery

In a short engagement, MögeTee went from a fragmented, low-engagement social presence to a growing community with 3,000+ new followers, 30+ active influencer partners, and its first nationally coordinated seasonal campaign.
