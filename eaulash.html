Overview

A lash brand, built entirely from scratch

Eau De Lash is a beauty e-commerce brand I created from the ground up — not just the marketing, but the product itself. I identified the target market (women 18–35), sourced an innovative product from China, and built the entire brand identity including logo, product packaging, social media templates, and color palette.

The project combined entrepreneurship with digital marketing — I had to think like both a founder and a strategist, launching simultaneously across a Shopify store, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.

The product itself was the differentiator: an all-in-one adhesive eyeliner that doubles as lash glue — an innovative 1-step solution that didn't exist from most competitors at the time.

The Challenge

Breaking into a saturated beauty market

01

Dominant Competitors

The false lash market was dominated by established brands — DoeBaby, HouseOfLash, Glamnetic — with loyal audiences and large ad budgets. Standing out required a genuinely different value proposition.

02

Zero Starting Point

No brand, no audience, no product — everything had to be built simultaneously. I had to source the right product, create the brand identity, design packaging, and launch marketing all at once.

03

Product-Market Fit

Finding a product niche that would resonate with women 18–35 while offering something new — not just another lash brand, but a smarter, easier solution for everyday wear.

04

Multi-Channel Growth

Building organic reach across Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok while running paid ads and recruiting influencers — all with a lean budget and no existing follower base.

The Approach

Four pillars to launch and grow

Pillar 01
Brand Identity & Product Innovation
  • Sourced a unique adhesive eyeliner + lash glue hybrid product from China
  • Designed full brand kit: logo, color palette, packaging, social media templates
  • Built a Shopify e-commerce store with product photography and copy
  • Positioned Eau De Lash as a smarter, 1-step alternative to traditional lash routines
Pillar 02
Aesthetic Content Creation
  • Produced beauty-forward content tailored to women 18–35 on Instagram and TikTok
  • Created reels, tutorials, and product demos aligned with trending beauty aesthetics
  • Maintained a consistent visual brand identity across all platforms
  • Used Canva and editing tools to produce high-quality assets on a lean budget
Pillar 03
Paid Advertising & Social Growth
  • Launched targeted Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns to drive discovery
  • Ran A/B tests on creative and audience segments to maximize ROI
  • Built organic follower base to 1,300+ within the first few months post-launch
  • Used social media management tools to schedule and monitor performance
Pillar 04
Affiliate & Influencer Marketing
  • Recruited micro-influencers in the beauty niche for product seeding and reviews
  • Set up an affiliate program to incentivize word-of-mouth referrals
  • Coordinated content creation and posting schedules with influencer partners
  • Leveraged creator audiences to drive targeted traffic to the Shopify store
