The Company

A Global Brand Entering a New Market

UPPF is a globally recognized leader in Paint Protection Film, with an active presence across 85+ countries. After three years of building their US footprint, the brand had successfully established their Clear PPF lineup and developed a dealership network recognized throughout the automotive industry.

As internal restructuring triggered a full company rebranding, UPPF needed to navigate a critical transition — launching a new brand identity, a new product line, and a new market strategy simultaneously, without disrupting the partner relationships they had worked hard to build.

The Challenge

Everything Changing at Once

UPPF entered their rebranding period facing four major pressure points simultaneously — each one capable of derailing momentum on its own.

Complete Brand Overhaul

New website, new logo, and a full visual identity redesign — with no room for a gradual rollout. Everything needed to launch together, cohesively.

65+ New Products to Launch

An entirely new Color PPF line required professional photography, optimized descriptions, and all-new sales materials — built from scratch.

Partner Disruption

Existing dealers and distributors carried legacy products and old branding. Transitioning them smoothly — without losing partnerships — was critical to the brand's future.

Invisible to End Customers

UPPF had strong B2B recognition with shop owners but almost no awareness among the car owners who actually purchase PPF — a ceiling that limited long-term demand growth.

The Approach

Three Pillars of Execution

01

Execute the Rebrand Flawlessly

Rebuilt uppf-usa.com from the ground up on Zoho Sites. Created 70 new product images to support the full Color PPF line launch. Designed all supporting marketing collateral — brochures, banners, and sales documents — to give partners everything they needed to represent the new brand. Launched coordinated social media content, paid ad campaigns, and email campaigns to announce the rebrand to both trade partners and consumers.

🖥️ New website · 70 product assets created · Full collateral suite · Email & paid ad launch campaigns
02

Win Over End Customers

Shifted paid ad targeting from a B2B focus to consumer pain points — "PPF saves you thousands in long-term paint damage" and "window tint shields you from the Texas heat." Produced Instagram Reels in plain, relatable language designed for everyday car owners rather than industry insiders. One Reel broke through organically, reaching 20K+ views and triggering a 3× spike in sales that month — proving the strategy worked. The automotive niche demands visually compelling content, and every piece was designed to match that standard.

🎬 1 viral Reel → 20K+ views → 3× monthly sales in a single month
03

Build a Scalable Marketing Infrastructure

Developed a complete marketing funnel with promotional calendars, brand protocols, and standardized internal documentation — so every campaign, post, and partner communication aligned with the new brand identity. This system gave the team a repeatable, consistent process that would continue to deliver results as the brand scaled its US presence.

📋 Marketing funnel · Promotional calendar · Brand protocols · Internal documentation library

UPPF completed its rebranding with a fully cohesive digital presence, a complete 70-asset product library, and a marketing system built to scale. The national dealer network grew to 400+, existing partners were retained through a disruptive transition, and the brand gained its first meaningful end-consumer audience.

Most notably, a single Instagram Reel produced entirely in-house drove 20K+ organic views and triggered a 3× sales month — proving that the right message, aimed at the right audience, can move the needle fast even in a niche B2B industry.
